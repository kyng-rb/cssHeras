Descendent selectors - Represents the union of two or more elements joined by an blank space, apply styles to the
second element that have direct related to the first. example 

div h2 {...}

the above example apply styles to the all h2 elements inside a div tag

take in account that is not necesary an direct relation between the elements like son and father, it mean that with 
the same example, the h2 elements can be inside other element and still applying the styles
